---
title: Audience Operations
---

Audience operations in Aletheia sit on top of audience state owned by Kairos.

Useful dashboard flows typically include:

- finding an audience quickly
- understanding why members qualify
- reviewing recent changes
- handing the audience off to another team or downstream workflow

Keep the boundary clear in the docs:

- Kairos defines and persists the audience logic.
- Aletheia helps people inspect and operate around that logic.
