---
title: Dashboard Overview
---

Aletheia is the dashboard for Kairos.

It is where teams inspect what the platform knows, review audience state, and move through operational workflows without dropping down to raw API calls.

## What Aletheia should optimize for

- fast visibility into profile and audience state
- discoverable workflows for non-platform users
- shared views for analytics, lifecycle, and operations teams
- confidence that what users see maps back to Kairos as the source of truth

## What Aletheia is not

Aletheia is not a separate data platform. It should not introduce a competing customer model or business vocabulary that drifts away from Kairos.
