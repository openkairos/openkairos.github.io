---
title: Analytics and Insights Overview
---

Applies to: `Kairos and Aletheia`

Analytics is another cross-cutting capability.

Kairos provides the data model and the underlying customer state. Aletheia provides the dashboard views and exploration workflows that help teams answer questions quickly.

Document the capability in two parts:

- what the platform computes, stores, or exposes
- what the dashboard helps users inspect or compare
