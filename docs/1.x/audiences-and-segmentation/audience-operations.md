---
title: Audience Operations
---

Applies to: `Kairos and Aletheia`

Audience operations span both products.

Kairos defines and persists the audience state. Aletheia helps people find that audience, inspect membership, review changes, and act on operational questions around it.

Useful workflows typically include:

- finding an audience quickly
- understanding why members qualify
- reviewing changes over time
- handing audience context to another team or workflow
