---
title: Profiles and Identity
---

Kairos combines known and anonymous activity into unified customer profiles.

## Identity resolution

The identity layer is responsible for connecting multiple identifiers into a durable view of the same customer or account. In practice that means linking records such as:

- user IDs
- emails
- device identifiers
- session identifiers
- CRM identifiers

## Profiles

A profile should be treated as a product-facing asset, not just a database row. It becomes the shared surface used by segmentation, analytics, and activation flows.

Profile data may include:

- static attributes
- recent and historical activity
- lifecycle state
- consent and governance metadata
- segment memberships
- derived scores or traits
