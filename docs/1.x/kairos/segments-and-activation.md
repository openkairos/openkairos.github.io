---
title: Segments and Activation
---

Segments turn customer data into action.

## Segments

Segments can be defined from:

- profile attributes
- behavioral activity
- recency and frequency rules
- derived scores
- combinations of user and account state

## Activation

Once a segment exists, Kairos can make it available to downstream systems and workflows. The exact channel or execution surface may differ, but the platform concern stays the same:

- determine who qualifies
- expose that decision reliably
- let downstream systems act on it

## Relationship with Aletheia

Kairos owns the segment logic and the underlying audience state. Aletheia gives teams a visual surface for inspecting the audience, checking related metrics, and operating around the resulting data.
